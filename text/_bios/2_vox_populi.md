# Vox Populi

Vox Populi doesn't come with easy words to describe her, but her M.A.R.S. bio of "Supermodel, truthseeker, intellectual" certainly gives a foundation. Denizen obsession with the glamour star of Vox Omnia Rex continues to justify itself thanks to Populi's boundless style and web of connections, letting her open the eyes of the public to the hottest takes, fresh from the mouths of faction leaders and the ritziest individuals Navis Caeli has to offer.
